Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can CredO be deployed?

CredO can typically be deployed within 2-4 weeks, depending on your infrastructure complexity and customization requirements. Our team provides full implementation support.

What support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 technical support, dedicated customer success managers, comprehensive documentation, and training programs for your team.

Is CredO compliant with industry standards?

Yes, CredO is compliant with SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, and other major compliance frameworks. We regularly undergo third-party audits.

Can CredO integrate with existing security tools?

Absolutely. CredO integrates with 200+ security tools including SIEMs, firewalls, endpoint protection, and cloud security platforms through APIs and connectors.

What is the pricing model?

CredO offers flexible pricing based on the number of devices, users, or data volume. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you offer on-premise deployment?

Yes, CredO is available as a cloud service, on-premise deployment, or hybrid configuration to meet your specific security and compliance requirements.
