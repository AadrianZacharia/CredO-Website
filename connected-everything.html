IoT Security Challenges

Device Vulnerabilities

Many IoT devices lack basic security features like encryption, authentication, and secure boot processes.

Impact: High Risk

Network Complexity

Heterogeneous networks with mixed protocols make monitoring and securing IoT traffic extremely challenging.

Impact: Medium Risk

Lack of Visibility

Organizations often don't know what IoT devices are on their networks or how they're communicating.

Impact: Critical Risk

Update Management

Many IoT devices are difficult or impossible to update, leaving them vulnerable to known exploits.

Impact: High Risk
